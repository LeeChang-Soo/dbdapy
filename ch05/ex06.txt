theta: the probability for head
p(theta=.25) = .25
p(theta=.5)  = .5
p(theta=.75) = .25

D = 3 heads and 9 tails

p(D) = sum(p(D|theta) * p(theta) for theta in model)

p(D|theta=.25) = .25 ^ 3 * .75 ^ 9 = 0.0011731982
p(D|theta=.5) = .5 ^ 3 * .5 ^ 9 = .5 ^ 12 = 0.0002441406
p(D|theta=.75) = .75 ^ 3 * .25 ^ 3 = 0.0000016093
p(D) = 0.0004157722

p(theta|D) = p(D|theta) * p(theta) / p(D)

p(theta=.25|D) = 0.7054333023
p(theta=.5|D)  = 0.2935990252
p(theta=.75|D) = 0.0009676726
